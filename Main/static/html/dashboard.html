"Search Popularity Index" (SPI) measures the frequency of our
specified search term in the top "trending Google searches".

For example, an SPI for 100 for the game "Apex" would mean 100%
of of the top trending Google searches contained "Apex".

NOTE: Values with 0 do not appear on the map.
